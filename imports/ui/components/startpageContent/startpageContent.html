What is EasyParty?

EasyParty is an event planning tool for private use. You can use it to plan your birthday, a wedding or a simple barbecue. It is a so called one-stop-shop where you have all important information together. So you can never misplace your notes again! Additionally, our different categories will help you to stay organized. Other features like the progress bar or the open tasks will show you the current state of your planning phase immediately. You are not planning the event alone? No problem at all!
